HEALTH NOTES - HOME

Welcome to my homepage! Health Notes is a public forum targeting healthcare practitioners and providers of all levels of education and experience, encouraging free exchange of ideas using a post format, regarding a broad spectrum of conditions and treatments affecting individuals.

TO UTILIZE THE SITE, FIRST GO TO THE PRACTITIONER SIGN-IN FORM BELOW ON THIS PAGE AND COMPLETE THE REQUIRED FIELDS THEN SUBMIT TO GAIN ACCESS TO THE PORTAL PAGE.

FROM THE PORTAL PAGE, AS AN EXAMPLE, GO TO POSTS AND COMPLETE THE REQUIRED FIELDS, ADDING WHAT YOU LIKE, AND SUBMIT.

After submitting, click on Re-Enter Credentials/Return Home and this will take you back to the homepage. From here you can go to the Posts link and click, it will take you to your most recent post, and a list of all prior posts in chronological order, which you can update or delete. The other options function similarly.

I hope you find the information on the site informative, useful and perhaps even life saving.
