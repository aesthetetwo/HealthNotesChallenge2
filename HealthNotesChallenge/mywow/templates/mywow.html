HEALTH NOTES CHALLENGE - HOME

Welcome to my homepage! Health Notes Challenge is a public forum targeting healthcare practitioners and providers of all levels of education and experience, encouraging free exchange of ideas using a post format, regarding a broad spectrum of conditions and treatments affecting individuals. To utilize the site, first go to the sign in form at the bottom of this page and complete the required fields and submit to gain access to the portal page. From the portal page, choice from the options Practitioner, Conditions, Treatments and Post. As an example, go to Post and complete the required fields, adding what you like and submit. After submitting, click on re-submit credentials and this will take you back to the homepage. From here you can go to the Posts link and click, it will take you to your most recent post, which you can update or delete. We hope you find the information on the site useful!
